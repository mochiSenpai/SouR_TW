    I. First Page

    When a user acces this aplication he has 2 choices: if he has an account he will introduce his username and his password or, if he doesn't have an account he will complete a form in order to create an account.

    II. Map Page

    After the user is logged he can choose the country where he will travel

    III. Choices Page

    When he chooses a country he will automatically be redirected to a page where he will be able to choose the kind of person he is looking gifts for(he will enter the age, gender and also the month when he will travel). By knowing this characteristics our application can provide more precise recommandations.

    IV. Results Page

    On this page the user will see the person souvenirs for who he's looking for with a minimal description and an aproximated price. If he likes one of this souvenirs he can add it on his profile page by pressing the button "I'd like that".

    V. Profile Page

    The users have the posibility to view which countries they have been selected and which souvenirs on their profile pages. There will also be the informations about themselves they have entered when they created the account.
